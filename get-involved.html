Become a Volunteer

Share your time and skills to make a difference

At WWF, we believe that positive change happens when communities come together. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or join us for a one-time event, your contribution matters.

Education

Teach children at our Community Learning Centres, help with homework, organize educational activities.

Healthcare

Assist in organizing health camps, coordinate with medical professionals, help with patient registration.

Skills Training

Conduct vocational training sessions in tailoring, computer skills, mobile repair, or other trades.

Awareness

Help with advocacy campaigns, community outreach, street plays, and awareness generation programs.

Administration

Support with documentation, data entry, fundraising, grant writing, accounting, or general office work.

Documentation

Help with photography, videography, social media, content writing, and documenting impact stories.
